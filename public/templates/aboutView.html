Holibirthday
Holibirthday.com celebrates birthdays upstaged by holidays, on bad days or just forgotten. If your birthday falls anywhere in late December, for instance, if you get a present at all, it's going to be red and green with stupid bells, right? Hanukah? A dreidel. New Years? Warm champagne & cold pizza!
Born on Feb 29th, leap day? You've been really short-changed! Your deserve an annual birthday!
How about a birthday of a friend or family member that you totally forget every year?
Holibirthday is your catharsis! Pick a new date and we'll all celebrate!
Where did Holibirthday come from?
I created Holibirthday in 2006 as catharsis for my wife, born on Dec 31st. She hated the fact that her birthday was completely upstaged and all the places to celebrate it were booked up.
I made a T-shirt that captured her angst:
I then started thinking of all those who never really get to celebrate their birthdays because of bad days, tragedies, etc. like December 7th, November 22nd or September 11th. How about the same day your dog died?
Holibirthday celebrates you - not a tragic event that you weren't part of
Holibirthday, including a new birth date to pick if you choose, could be your best present ever!
Forget Sweetest Day, Grandparents Day, and all that made-up stuff. This will soon be a philanthropic generator, raising funds and celebrating my gift to you of your own Holibirthday, dedicated to your birthday, regardless of its previous upstaged date!
So, got a friend or relative who's a Holibirther? Tell them about Holibirthday! They'll likely thank you, saying how they always wished for this day, their day in the sun.
